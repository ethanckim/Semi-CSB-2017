Welcome to Ethan's Clash Royal Arena!

School Stuff: Ethan Kim, Period 4, Computer Studies B

Enter the Arena! My name is Ethan, and I will guide you through this website. This website is a unofficial guide for clash royale that provides tips, well structured decks, deck archtypes, and more!


Clash royale is a multiplayer game that uses the online match system to put you into battles with people with the same level and skills. By winning battles you can earn rewards, using it to expand your card collection and upgrading cards. Use these cards to make your battle deck! Each card has a unique effect, so try using new cards! You can also play with your friends if you are in the same clan or using facebook.
